Who Are We?

Sk8 Shop is an online skateboard and parts supplier. We sell completed build skateboards and the parts on their own individually.

Mission Statement:

We aim to provide a one-stop shop for complete skateboards and skateboard parts. Skateboarding is for everyone, and most people lack access to new boards or replacement parts. We aim to keep the process simple to get you on your board faster.
